<p>There’s no better way to celebrate the end of a hectic term than with
the roar of the Victorian spirit at Kallang! This year’s College Road Run
saw a sea of yellow descending upon OCBC Square, ready to take on the 3.6km
and 4.3km routes. Whether they were sprinting for a personal best or enjoying
a slow jog with friends, the laughter and camaraderie were palpable. As
the sun set on Term 1, our Victorians proved that they can go the distance
- together!</p>
