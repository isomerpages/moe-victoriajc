<p>The VJC Open House 2026 was a vibrant showcase of Victorian spirit, bringing
our theme “VJC is the place to be” to life across campus. The school was
alive with energy as prospective students and their families immersed themselves
in everything VJC has to offer. From dynamic performances to engaging,
hands-on experiences by our CCAs, every corner of the campus had something
exciting in store. Our subject booths welcomed enthusiastic visitors eager
to explore academic pathways, while dedicated students and teachers created
a warm and supportive atmosphere for all. More than just an open house,
it was a celebration of community, discovery, and the VJC experience —
where every future Victorian knows they will always have a place to belong.</p>
