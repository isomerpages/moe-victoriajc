<p>The annual JC1 Principal-Parents gave the parents of our JC1 students
a front-row seat to the VJC experience, laying the foundation for a strong
partnership in the two-year journey ahead. During the session, they explored
our holistic programmes and the diverse support systems available for their
children. From the heartfelt advice shared by JC2 seniors to the strategic
insights from the JC1 Year Head, the session bridged the gap between home
and school. The evening concluded with a lively Q&amp;A panel, where school
leaders engaged in an open dialogue with parents, ensuring every family
left empowered as their children embark on this exciting chapter.</p>
