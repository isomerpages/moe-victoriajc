<p>VJC charged into the Year of the Horse with unstoppable energy! The celebrations
kicked off with a lively Lo Hei, as staff tossed high for galloping good
fortune, prosperity, and success in the year ahead. The excitement continued
with a high-spirited concert, featuring dynamic performances by the Chinese
Orchestra, Malay Cultural Society and Wushu. With energy running wild and
festive cheer in full stride, it was a joyous and memorable way for Victorians
to usher in the Lunar New Year. Huat ah!</p>
