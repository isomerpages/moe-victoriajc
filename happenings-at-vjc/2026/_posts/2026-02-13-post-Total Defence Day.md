<p>Victorians commemorated Total Defence Day 2026 alongside the rest of the
nation by taking part in Exercise SG Ready, with a focus on power outage
preparedness. Through this experience, students gained a better understanding
of how daily life can be affected when electricity is disrupted. As part
of the programme, students explored how Singapore’s national power grid
supports everyday needs, while recognising that outages can still occur
despite its reliability. They also learned practical ways to be equipped
to handle power outages anytime, anywhere — both as individuals and as
a community, with many having lessons in the open. The exercise served
as a meaningful reminder of the importance of readiness and resilience,
empowering students to stay prepared in the face of unexpected disruptions.</p>
