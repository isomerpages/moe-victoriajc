<p>Victorians commemorated Total Defence Day 2026 by engaging in a dialogue
session with two of our alumni - Commissioner of Police, Mr How Kwang Hwee
and Deputy Commissioner (Strategy and Corporate Services) of SCDF, Mr Teong
How Hwa. During the dialogue, JC2 students reflected on resilience, vigilance
and the role each of them plays in safeguarding Singapore’s future. Alongside
the rest of the nation, Victorians also took part in Exercise SG Ready,
with a focus on power outage preparedness. As part of the programme, students
explored how Singapore’s national power grid supports everyday needs, while
recognising that outages can still occur despite its reliability. They
also learned practical ways to be equipped to handle power outages anytime,
anywhere — both as individuals and as a community, with many having lessons
in the open. The exercise served as a meaningful reminder of the importance
of readiness and resilience, empowering students to stay prepared in the
face of unexpected disruptions.</p>
