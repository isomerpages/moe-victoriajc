<p>VJC’s iGlobe, with the support of the People’s Association, hosted the
Singapore Model Cabinet (SMC) 2024 during the March holidays. Graced by
Ms Jamie Ko, Director of Regional Affairs and Policy from Grab (Singapore),
and Mr Seah Kian Peng, Speaker of Parliament, the event saw more than 250
students from different schools debating on various issues with poise and
intellect, setting the stage for the leaders of tomorrow!</p>
