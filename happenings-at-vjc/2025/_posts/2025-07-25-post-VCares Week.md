<p>Amid the hustle and bustle of Term 3, VJC students paused to focus on
mental wellness during <em>VCares Week</em>. Through engaging talks by alumni,
hands-on workshops, and interactive booths by partner organisations, Victorians
were reminded of the importance of caring for their own mental health while
supporting one another in everyday life.</p>
