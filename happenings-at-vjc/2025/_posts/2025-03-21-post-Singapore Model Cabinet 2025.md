<p>VJC’s iGlobe, with the support of the People’s Association, hosted the
Singapore Model Cabinet (SMC) 2025 during the March holidays. Graced by
Mr Tan See Leng, Minister for Manpower and Second Minister for Trade and
Industry, and Mr Seah Kian Peng, Speaker of Parliament, the event saw more
than 170 students from different schools debating on various issues with
poise and intellect, setting the stage for the leaders of tomorrow.</p>
