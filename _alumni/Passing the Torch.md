The Victoria we know today stands on the shoulders of those who came before, their dedication and commitment to this school reflected in every chapter of our story.

We are grateful for the support of our alumni, parents, and well-wishers who have chosen to be part of our journey. Your contributions have enabled VJC to enhance our programmes and facilities, and extend support to deserving students who need it most.

Thank you for your partnership in providing holistic education to all Victorians. If you wish to get in touch with us, you are welcome to reach out at [victoria\_jc@moe.edu.sg](mailto:victoria_jc@moe.edu.sg).
