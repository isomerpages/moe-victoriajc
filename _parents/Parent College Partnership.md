### Parent-College Partnership

It takes a community to educate a child. In VJC, we recognise the importance of partnering parents and the community to create a holistic learning environment for our students. We believe that working with parents is one of the best ways to support learning and provide the best educational experience for our students. 

![](/images/Resources-for-Parents-2-1024x425.jpg)
